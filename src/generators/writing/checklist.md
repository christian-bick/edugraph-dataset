### Writing Visual Checklist
- **Layout Order:** MUST BE Ten-frame (left) -> Number Label (middle) -> Writing Boxes (right).
- **Ten-Frame Pre-fill:** The ten-frame must be pre-filled with blue dots matching the number in both Modes.
- **Trace Consistency:** In `outline: true`, the grey trace number must be visible in Question mode. In `outline: false`, the boxes must be empty in Question mode.
