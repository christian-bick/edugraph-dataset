### Place Value Teen Pedagogical Checklist
- **Range:** Numbers must be strictly in the range [11, 19].
- **Modes:** Must support both 'compose-teen' (tens/ones to target) and 'decompose-teen' (target to tens/ones) mathematical structures.
- **Ten-Frame Representation:** Visual representation must use two ten-frames, with the first ten-frame fully filled (representing 10) and the second ten-frame filled with the remaining ones.
