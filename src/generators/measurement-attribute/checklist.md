### Measurement Attribute Visual Checklist

- The problem must ask what attribute (length, height, or weight) is being measured with the shown setup.
- The visual illustration must represent the selected attribute:
  - For length, a pencil with a horizontal dimension line.
  - For height, a tree with a vertical dimension line.
  - For weight, a dial scale with a box on top.
- The solution must highlight the button corresponding to the correct attribute.
