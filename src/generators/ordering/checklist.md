### Ordering Visual Checklist
- **Arrow Alignment:** The diagonal arrow (↗ or ↘) must correctly point from the unordered set to the ordered boxes.
- **Sorting Logic:** Ascending (↗) must result in lowest to highest. Descending (↘) must result in highest to lowest.
- **Horizontal Flow:** The entire problem (unordered set, arrow, ordered set) must be displayed in a single horizontal row.
