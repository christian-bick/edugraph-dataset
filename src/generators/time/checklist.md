### Time Visual Checklist
- **Hand Alignment:** Hour and minute hands must point accurately based on the time.
- **Clock Face:** All 12 hour marks and 60 minute marks must be correctly rendered.
- **Coloring (Normal):** Hands are black. Digital box is empty in Q, green in A.
- **Coloring (Reverse):** Digital box text is black. Clock hands are hidden in Q, green in A.
