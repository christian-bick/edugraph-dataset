### Measurement Visual Checklist
- **Label Clipping:** All ruler labels (especially 0 and the max value) must be fully visible and not clipped.
- **Color Consistency:** In Normal mode, the rectangle must be SteelBlue and the answer box green (A-mode). In Reverse mode, the rectangle must be green (A-mode) and the answer box text black.
- **Marker Alignment:** Ticks on the ruler must be correctly spaced and aligned with the numeric labels.
