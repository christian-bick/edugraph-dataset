### Counting Classify Sort Pedagogical Checklist
- **Items:** Renders a list of mixed shapes and colors.
- **Classification:** Categorizes items strictly by 'shape' or 'color'.
- **Sorting Relation:** Determines which category has the 'most' or 'least' number of items.
- **Ties Filtering:** Ensures no ties exist for the target answer category to prevent ambiguous solutions.
