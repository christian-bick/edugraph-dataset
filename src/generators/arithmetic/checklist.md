### Arithmetic Visual Checklist
- **Box Centering:** Numbers and symbols must be perfectly centered within their respective boxes.
- **Dynamic Blanks:** The specific part identified in the filename (e.g., `blankPart-num1`) must be the one hidden in Question mode and highlighted in Answer mode.
- **Vertical Alignment:** In `operations-vertical`, digits must be aligned by their place value (ones under ones, tens under tens).
- **Symbol Visibility:** The operator symbol (+, -, ×, ÷) must be clearly visible and not overlapping with the numbers.
