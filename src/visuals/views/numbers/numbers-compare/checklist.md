### Numbers Compare View Checklist

#### Visual Layout & Rendering Requirements
1. **Instruction / Problem Layout:**
   - The comparison exercise must display `num1` on the left, an answer input/box in the middle, and `num2` on the right in a clear, large monospace font.
2. **Solution/Answer Options:**
   - In **Question Mode (`_mode-Q`)**, the middle answer box must be empty/blank with a clean border.
   - In **Solution Mode (`_mode-S`)**, the correct comparison symbol (`<`, `>`, or `=`) must render in the middle box in emerald green.
